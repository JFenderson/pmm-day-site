About PMM Day

The 1st Annual PMM Day/weekend is a weekend of events showcasing the family portion of the long standing Miles College Purple Maching Machine and their family and friends. The main purpose of this event is to create a tradition that bridges the gaps between old-school band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Arthur Means from 1996-2006, the more current band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Donald Crawford from 2007-2012 and/or Mr.James Merriweather from 2012-2016, and teh current members of the Purple Marching Machine, under the direction of Mr. Willie Snipes.

Some background of the Purple Marching Machine. This HBCU marching was started in 1996 by Mr. Arthur Means. During his tenure directing teh band, They have performed many events such as The Macy Day Parade and The Kentucky Derby. The band is mostly known for it's high-energy halftime performance and gospel collaborations within their theme with the shcool's tie-ins with the CME(Christian Methodist Episcopal) church. Most recently the band was invited to do a halftime performance for the Atlanta Falcons and also was invited back to Atlanta for teh Honda Battle of the Bands Invitational Showcase.
