About PMM Day

The 1st Annual PMM Day/weekend is a weekend of events showcasing the family portion of the long standing Miles College Purple Maching Machine and their family and friends. The main purpose of this event is to create a tradition that bridges the gaps between old-school band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Arthur Means from 1996-2006, the more current band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Donald Crawford from 2007-2012 and/or Mr.James Merriweather from 2012-2016, and teh current members of the Purple Marching Machine, under the direction of Mr. Willie Snipes.

PMM Picnic

PMM Picnic
Saturday, 4/17 location: TBA

The first annual PMM Picnic will be held on a Saturday and location wil be given at a later date. We will have an opening booth when you get to the park to grab you wrist band for those the pre-ordered and a line for those who need a wristband. The event is open and free but food and drinks will only be given to those that possess one of the wrist band colors(white, purple, or gold)

Please be mindful of those that have volunteered to make sure this event is a success so please be patient when it comes to waiting on food and drinks. We wanted the PMM Family to not be bared to serving so everything is catered by a vendor. This is also a 5 drink limit but you are more than welcome to bring your own beverages as well to the picnic.
